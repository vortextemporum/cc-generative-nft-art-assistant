How to Play

Drive: Select a train by clicking, then use arrow keys or WASD to control speed and direction.

Switches: Click the arrow indicators at junctions to change track routing.

Dispatch: Press N to add new trains to the network.

Collisions: If trains crash, they'll reset after a moment.
