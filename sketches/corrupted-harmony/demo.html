CORRUPTED HARMONY

Effect & Style Reference

Shader Effects

Clean

No shader effect. Standard Lambert material with basic lighting. Used as baseline for comparison.

Dither

Bayer matrix dithering creates a retro pixel aesthetic. 4x4 ordered dithering pattern based on luminance.

Glitch

RGB channel shifting, scanlines, and random block corruption. Vertex displacement creates horizontal slice offset. Time-animated for dynamic movement.

Corrupt

Screen-space noise patterns with 6 algorithms:

voronoi perlin worley value ridged turbulence

Liquify

Wave distortion in vertex shader. Buildings appear to melt with dripping effects at base. Time-animated undulation.

Stencil

Posterization to 3-5 color levels. Creates flat, graphic novel aesthetic. Lighting still affects base shading.

Corrupt Shader - Noise Types

Voronoi

Cellular patterns with distinct regions.
- Bright red edges between cells
- Some cells inverted (white on dark)
- Some cells color-shifted

Perlin

Smooth gradient noise.
- Cyan bleeding in high areas
- Magenta bleeding in low areas
- Organic channel shifting

Worley

Cracked glass effect (F2-F1 distance).
- Dark or bright crack lines
- Orange glow near cracks
- Shattering appearance

Value

Posterized color bands.
- 4-color palette mapping
- Dark blue, red, cyan, cream
- Stepped transitions

Ridged

Glowing circuit veins.
- Orange/yellow vein lines
- Dark between veins
- Electronic/organic hybrid

Turbulence

Chaotic data corruption.
- RGB channel splitting
- Random area inversions
- Blocky artifacts

Architectural Styles

Brutalist

Heavy concrete blocks with horizontal ledges. Rooftop mechanical equipment boxes. Raw, imposing forms.

Deco

Art Deco setbacks with ornate spires. 4-tier stepped profile. Decorative trim at each level.

Gothic

Pointed roofs with corner pinnacles. Medieval cathedral inspired. Vertical emphasis.

Modernist

Glass curtain wall with frame lines. Transparent appearance. Rooftop antenna elements.

Retro

Retro-futurist bulging forms. Cylindrical segments with varying width. Layered dome tops.

Geometric

Pure geometric forms. Pyramid or crystal cluster tops. Simple base with complex crown.

Organic

Twisted bio-form tower. Progressive rotation and wobble. Blob-shaped top element.

Parametric

Grasshopper-inspired attractor deformation. Lofted floor plates with varying scale. Facade panel dividers.

Twisted

Turning Torso style progressive rotation. 90-270 degree total twist. Structural core and edge beams.

Voronoi

Facade panels in 7 styles: hexGrid, diamonds, triangles, circles, fins, louvers, mixed. Corner mullions and diagonal bracing.

Weirdness Levels

Subtle (12%)

Minimal distortions. Mostly normal buildings with occasional float or tilt.

Moderate (35%)

Noticeable but not overwhelming. Some floating, tilting, scaling.

Chaotic (60%)

Heavy distortion. Multiple weirdness types per building. Melt effects.

Reality-Collapse (85%)

Maximum weirdness. Inverted buildings, fragments, echoes. Full chaos.
